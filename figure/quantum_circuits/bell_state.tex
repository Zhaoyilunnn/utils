Prepare the Bell state $\ket{\Phi^+} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(\ket{00}+\ket{11})$ from $\ket{00}$.

1. Apply $H\otimes I$ to $\ket{00}$.
\[
(H\otimes I)\ket{00}
= \left(\frac{\ket{0}+\ket{1}}{\sqrt{2}}\right)\otimes \ket{0}
= \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(\ket{00}+\ket{10}).
\]

2. Apply CNOT with qubit 1 as control and qubit 2 as target.
\[
\operatorname{CNOT}\,\ket{00}=\ket{00},\quad \operatorname{CNOT}\,\ket{10}=\ket{11}.
\]
Thus,
\[
\operatorname{CNOT}\,\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(\ket{00}+\ket{10})
= \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(\ket{00}+\ket{11})
= \ket{\Phi^+}.
\]
